
Ascii for Letters
--------------------
small letters : a-97 to z-122
Capital letters : A-65 to  Z-90

97 (for 'a') - 65 (for 'A') = 32
small letter ascii  - caps ascii is 32

Numeric Difference between  Capital and small letter is 32  , that is small letter ascii  minus 32 gives capital letter ascii

For example: 97(a) -32 = 65(A)
